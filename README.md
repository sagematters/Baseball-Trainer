# Baseball Trainer

A phone-first daily training app for earning the starting SS/3B job at Truett McConnell by Spring 2027.

**Current phase:** Vacation Bridge (5 days/week + a daily guided mobility flow).

## What's in here

- **`index.html`** — the whole app. It's a single self-contained file (all the workouts, the exercise guides, the drawn figures, the mobility-flow player, and the daily-adjust buttons live inside it). No internet or setup needed to run it — just open it in any browser.

## How to use it

Open `index.html` in a browser, or visit the live site (see below). It opens straight to today's workout. Tap **Home / Gym / Field** to match your equipment, tap a feeling if your body's off, tap any exercise for the full how-to, and hit **Start the flow** for the daily mobility session.

## How to edit it

Everything is in `index.html`. Two easy ways to change it:

1. **On the GitHub website** — click `index.html`, click the pencil ✏️ icon, make your change, and click **Commit changes**. The live site updates in about a minute.
2. **Ask Claude** — describe what you want changed and Claude edits `index.html` for you.

The workouts are defined in the `DAYS` section of the file, the mobility sequence in the `FLOW` list, and every exercise's instructions in the `EX` list — all near the bottom, in plain text you can read.
